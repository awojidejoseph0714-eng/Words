Why does this exist?

This tool was built to simulate a single, crucial moment in creative work: the mental leap. A copywriter is constantly asked to connect a boring product feature (like "Steel") with a deep human emotion (like "Safety") under a big brand idea (like "Family"). This tool is a gym for that exact muscle.

What It Does & The Benefits

It's a "creative reflex trainer." By forcing you to make rapid, unrelated connections, it helps you:

  • Build Mental Flexibility: Stop thinking in straight lines.
  • Warm Up Your Brain: Use it for 5 minutes before a big project.
  • Train Lateral Thinking: Find new pathways between ideas.
  • Practice "The Big Idea": Practice tying every concept back to a core theme.

How To Use It

  1. Your Theme is your brand mandate. It cannot change.
  2. Your Words are your random facts. They are disposable.
  3. Your Task is to find a story that connects the Words *back to* the Theme.
  4. Type your connection. Press Enter (without Shift) to log it and get a new pair.
  5. Click "New Word Pair" or "New Theme" at any time.
